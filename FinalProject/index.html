Mote Lone (Sticker Design)

I designed stickers inspired by "Mote Lone," a traditional dessert enjoyed during Myanmar's Thingyan Festival. Mote Lone consists of sticky rice balls filled with sweet ingredients like jaggery and coconut, wrapped in banana leaves.

My stickers capture the essence of Mote Lone, celebrating its flavors and cultural significance in the vibrant spirit of Thingyan.

CUNY Commencement 2024 (GIF Design)

I created a GIF file for the CUNY Commencement 2024. The GIF captures the essence of the event, celebrating the achievements of graduates in a dynamic and engaging format.

It serves as a visual representation of the excitement and pride surrounding this significant milestone in the graduates' academic journey.

36 Days of Type (Typography Design)

I participated in "36 Days of Type," a creative challenge where artists worldwide design and share letters and numbers each day for 36 days. My project showcased my versatility and creativity as I explored different styles and techniques to represent each character uniquely.

It was a rewarding experience that allowed me to connect with a global community of designers and push the boundaries of my creativity.
